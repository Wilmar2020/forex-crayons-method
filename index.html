Each crayon color represents a proprietary confirmation market. The algorithm monitors all of them simultaneously on ONE chart and translates their relationships into simple color signals. You just read the colors.

BLUE

The fastest signal. First to react. Also identifies the parity level and acts as the system's manipulation detector. When BLUE diverges — the banks are at work.
GREEN
Early Confirmation
The leading indicator of direction. When BLUE and GREEN agree — a setup is forming. The GREEN confirmation leads the Euro cycle ~90% of the time.
GOLD
Gold Confirmation
Highly correlated to Euro cycles. When GOLD moves in the same direction as your trade — it confirms the move has real momentum behind it.
PINK
Volume Confirmation
Volume never lies. PINK confirms whether real institutional money is behind the move. Rising PINK = buyers in control. Falling PINK = sellers.
